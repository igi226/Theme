About Dependable Staffing Agency

The Best Temp Agency In Federal Way

The Best Temp Agency In Federal Way Dependable Staffing Agency is the best Temp Agency In Federal Way and is recognized as one of the most trusted Temp Agencies In Seattle, Washington.

Dependable Staffing Agency was founded in 1989 with a genuine desire to offer quality, compassionate, and professional skilled and non-skilled services. We are committed to providing a caring service that respects the personal values and dignity of our clients whom we serve in our community. We run a professional Temp Agency In Federal Way.

Our focus is to provide fully trained and qualified health professionals to meet high-quality staffing needs at hospitals, clinics, long-term care facilities, adult family homes, & adult day care centers along with providing home health services during the transition from independence to increased reliance upon others. Previously, Dependable Staffing Agency’s services had mainly been geared toward the medical industry. We are proud to announce that we are now providing staffing services for a multitude of industries outside of the medical field and we are recognized as one the best Temp Agencies In Seattle. Please browse our “Who We Hire“ section to see other industries in which we may serve you to meet your business needs!

Why choose Us?

We are committed to working as a team player of the facility wherever and whenever we are needed. Our professionals will follow the policies and procedures of the facility. In addition, we provide solutions for the entire employment and business cycle including permanent, temporary and contract recruitment, employee assessment and selection along with job placement and consulting. Our combination of high-quality training and real-world experience helps equip all workers for success in their professional careers.
